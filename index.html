ABOUT

I'm a professional glass engraver and a football enthusiast.
My first experience with engraving was a good number of years back after high school. I remember struggling with finding the way to change my physical environment which, more often than not, was filled with blunt materials that posed a danger to us while we played in the fields outside our homes. We were always barefoot, I couldn't afford the spiked boots. So I invested my time into learning how to upcycle and recycle glass, repurposing it from maybe a wine bottle to a classy water glass that many prefer to not drink from but decorate their shelves and cellars with. I engrave all kinds of glass, and add a touch of wildlife to them. It's been a long journey, I learn everyday and just like my son's building blocks, You start piece by piece, nobody understands what you're remaking or repurposing them to but, tada... you have a zoo in your living room and a game park hanging from your veranda's ceiling.
Here's to building zoos with glass!
